Shipping and Delivery Policy

Order Fulfillment and Delivery

Once your order has been successfully processed and paid for, we will collect your contact details and delivery address.

Driver Assignment and Communication

We will assign a driver to deliver your order. Our driver will contact you upon arrival at your delivery address to arrange access to the property.

Verification and Confirmation

For security and verification purposes, our driver will present the original receipt as confirmation that they were sent by us. Please ensure that you verify the receipt and confirm that the items delivered match your order.
