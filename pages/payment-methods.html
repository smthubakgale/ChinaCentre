Payment Methods

Available Payment Options

We offer the following payment options for your convenience:

  • Credit/Debit Card (Visa, Mastercard, Amex)
  • EFT (Electronic Funds Transfer)
  • COD (Cash on Delivery)
  • Instant EFT

Please note that all payments are subject to our terms and conditions. By making a payment, you agree to our terms and conditions.

Credit/Debit Card Payments

We accept Visa, Mastercard, and Amex credit/debit cards. Our online payment system is secure and encrypted to protect your personal and financial information.

To make a credit/debit card payment, simply select the credit/debit card option during checkout and follow the prompts to enter your card details.

EFT Payments

COD Payments

Cash on Delivery is available for orders within our delivery area. Please note that COD payments are subject to a small handling fee.

To make a COD payment, simply select the COD option during checkout. Our driver will collect the payment from you when delivering your order.

Instant EFT Payments

Instant EFT allows you to pay online using your bank account. This payment method is secure, convenient, and eliminates the need to wait for EFT clearance.

To make an Instant EFT payment, simply select the Instant EFT option during checkout and follow the prompts to enter your bank details.

Payment Security

We take payment security seriously. Our online payment system is secure and encrypted to protect your personal and financial information.

We also comply with the Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard (PCI DSS) to ensure that your payment information is handled securely.
